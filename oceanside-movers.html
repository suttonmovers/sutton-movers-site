Camp Pendleton & the Military Community

Camp Pendleton is the largest Marine Corps base on the West Coast, and Oceanside is where most of its families live off-base. Sutton Movers works regularly with Marines and Navy personnel who are arriving on new orders, departing for their next duty station, or moving between on-base and off-base housing. We understand the unique timelines involved with PCS moves and can work around reporting dates and housing availability.

Oceanside’s Coastal Living

The neighborhoods near Oceanside Pier, the Strand, and South O have a distinct coastal character. Homes are close together, parking is at a premium, and loading zones may require coordination with the city. Our team scouts the area before move day to identify the best approach for truck placement and equipment access.

Growing Inland Communities

East Oceanside and the areas near Mission San Luis Rey have seen significant residential growth. Newer subdivisions with wide streets and larger homes present fewer access challenges but require crews experienced with two-story layouts, multiple flights of stairs, and large furniture pieces.
