Apartment Moves Across Los Angeles

More than half of Angelenos rent, and apartment moves are our bread and butter in LA. We deal with building management requirements daily: freight elevator reservations, loading dock time slots, parking permits from the city, and narrow hallways in older buildings. Whether it is a studio in Koreatown, a one-bedroom in Silver Lake, or a penthouse in Downtown LA, our crew arrives prepared with the right equipment and a plan that fits your building’s rules.

Hollywood, Burbank & the Entertainment District

Moving in Hollywood and Burbank often means navigating production schedules, street closures for filming, and dense residential neighborhoods with limited parking. Many of our clients in these areas work in the entertainment industry and need moves scheduled around irregular hours. We accommodate early morning and late evening moves to work around call times and production schedules.

Santa Monica, Venice & the Westside

Westside moves come with coastal considerations: permit parking zones, PCH traffic, and older residential buildings with unique access challenges. From the high-rises on Ocean Avenue to the bungalows of Venice, we tailor our approach to each property and coordinate with building management to ensure a smooth process.

Pasadena, Glendale & the Foothills

The foothill communities offer larger homes, steeper driveways, and the 210 freeway corridor. We move families throughout Pasadena, Glendale, La Canada Flintridge, and Altadena, bringing the right trucks and padding to protect hardwood floors, wrap grand pianos, and navigate multi-level homes.
