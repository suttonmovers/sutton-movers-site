San Diego Movers

Moving to or within America's Finest City? From the bustling streets of Downtown and the Gaslamp Quarter to the laid-back coastal communities of Pacific Beach, Ocean Beach, and Mission Beach, Sutton Movers provides dependable, full-service moving throughout San Diego and all of San Diego County.

San Diego is one of the most diverse cities in Southern California, and every neighborhood comes with its own character and its own moving challenges. In Hillcrest and North Park, many apartment buildings are older multi-story walk-ups without elevators, which means your movers need the experience and stamina to carry furniture up and down narrow stairwells safely. In University Heights, Normal Heights, South Park, and Golden Hill, tree-lined streets and tight parking can make truck positioning tricky. Downtown high-rises and condos near the Gaslamp often require parking permits, elevator reservations, and coordination with building management. Our crew handles all of these situations regularly and knows how to plan for them before move day.

The beach communities of Pacific Beach, Ocean Beach, Mission Beach, and La Jolla present their own set of logistics. Street parking is limited, alley access is common, and some properties sit on narrow lots with tight turns. Point Loma has steep hillside streets and homes with limited driveway access. Farther inland, neighborhoods like Clairemont, Mira Mesa, Kearny Mesa, Scripps Ranch, and Tierrasanta are more suburban but can still involve long driveways, gated communities, or HOA requirements. In communities like Barrio Logan and Logan Heights, we work around mixed residential and commercial zones where street access may be restricted at certain hours. No matter where your move takes you in San Diego, we have handled it before.

We serve every type of residential move in San Diego, from studio apartments and one-bedroom condos to large four-bedroom and five-bedroom homes. Whether you are a young professional moving into your first place in North Park, a growing family relocating to Scripps Ranch, or a retiree downsizing from a house in Point Loma to a smaller condo in La Jolla, our team scales to fit your needs. We bring the right crew size and equipment for every job, and we treat your belongings with the same care regardless of the size of the move.

San Diego is home to a large military community, and we are proud to serve active duty service members, veterans, and military families stationed at Camp Pendleton, MCAS Miramar, Naval Base San Diego, and Naval Base Point Loma. Military relocations often come with tight timelines and specific requirements, and we have the flexibility to accommodate PCS moves, short-notice relocations, and moves that coordinate with military housing offices. We also offer military discounts as a small thank-you for your service. For more on our military moving services, visit our Camp Pendleton movers page.

San Diego is also a major college town, with students moving in and out of housing near UCSD, San Diego State University, the University of San Diego, and Point Loma Nazarene University every year. We offer affordable labor-only options for students who already have a rental truck, as well as full-service moves with our own trucks and equipment. Whether you need help loading a U-Haul for a dorm move or a complete pack-and-move for an off-campus apartment, we can help.

Beyond residential moves, we handle commercial and office relocations throughout San Diego's business districts. From tech companies in Sorrento Valley and UTC to professional offices in Kearny Mesa and Downtown, we work around your business schedule to minimize downtime. We also offer junk removal services as an add-on for any move. If you have furniture, appliances, or other items you do not want to bring to your new place, we can haul them away on the same trip so you start fresh.

Sutton Movers is a family-owned moving company based in San Marcos, right here in San Diego County. We are not a franchise or a broker. When you call us, you talk to our team, and our crew shows up on move day. We are fully insured with general liability, workers' compensation, and commercial auto coverage, and we can provide a Certificate of Insurance to your apartment complex, HOA, or property manager with 48 hours' notice. Our pricing is transparent with no hidden fees, no fuel surcharges, and no surprise charges. That approach has earned us a 5-star rating across Google, Thumbtack, and the Better Business Bureau, and it is why San Diego residents continue to trust us with their moves.
