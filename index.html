Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide the truck, or is it labor-only?

+
We offer both. Full-service includes our truck, equipment, and crew. Labor-only means we provide the muscle — loading, unloading, packing — while you supply the truck (U-Haul, PODS, etc.). Just let us know what you need when you request a quote.

How far in advance should I book?

+
We recommend booking at least a week ahead, especially for weekend moves. That said, we handle last-minute moves regularly — sometimes even same day. Call us and we'll do our best to make it work.

What if my apartment complex requires a COI?

+
No problem. We're fully insured with general liability and workers' comp. We provide Certificates of Insurance (COI) within 72 hours of request — just send us your building's requirements and we'll handle it.

Do you offer military discounts?

+
Yes. We proudly offer discounts for active duty military and veterans. We serve many families around Camp Pendleton, Miramar, and the surrounding bases. Just mention your service when requesting a quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

+
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), Apple Pay, Zelle, Venmo, and cash. Payment is collected after the job is complete — no upfront deposits required.
