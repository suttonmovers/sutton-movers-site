Understanding Coronado’s Moving Challenges

Coronado is connected to the mainland by the iconic Coronado Bridge and the Silver Strand. Both routes present timing considerations for moving trucks. The bridge has height restrictions and can back up significantly during rush hours and summer weekends. The Silver Strand route through Imperial Beach adds distance but avoids bridge traffic entirely. Our team evaluates both options for every move and selects the approach that keeps your timeline intact.

Naval Base Coronado & Military Families

Home to Naval Air Station North Island, the Naval Amphibious Base, and several other commands, Coronado has one of the densest military populations in California. Sutton Movers has worked with dozens of military families navigating PCS moves, temporary housing setups, and the unique challenge of moving on and off base with gate access requirements. We offer a military discount and understand that orders do not wait.
