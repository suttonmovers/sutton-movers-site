Dana Point Movers

Planning a move in Dana Point? Perched on the bluffs above Dana Point Harbor, this coastal gem offers world-class whale watching, boutique shops, and ocean-view living. Sutton Movers provides dependable, full-service moving throughout Dana Point and South Orange County. Whether you're relocating to a home in Monarch Beach, settling into a condo in Niguel Shores, or moving out of an apartment in Lantern Village, our team knows the area and can handle every detail of your move.

Dana Point's neighborhoods each come with their own character and their own moving challenges. The Strand and Headlands feature hillside properties with steep driveways and limited truck access, while Capistrano Beach has narrow coastal roads that require careful navigation. Many of the condo and townhome communities throughout Dana Point, including those near the harbor, have strict HOA move-in and move-out requirements with reserved elevator times, loading dock schedules, and certificate of insurance documentation. Our crew is experienced with all of these logistics and will coordinate with your property management to make sure everything goes smoothly.

Sutton Movers offers both full-service moves and labor-only options for Dana Point residents. Full-service means we bring the truck, moving blankets, dollies, and all the labor needed to get your belongings from point A to point B. If you already have a truck or container and just need professional movers to load and unload, our labor-only service is a great fit. Either way, you get the same careful handling and professional team.

We also handle a high volume of apartment and condo moves in Dana Point's beachside communities. Multi-story buildings, tight hallways, and shared elevators are part of the job for us. Our movers know how to protect floors, walls, and door frames while working efficiently in compact spaces. If your building requires padding in the elevator or specific move-in hours, we will take care of it.

Located between San Clemente to the south and Laguna Niguel to the north, Dana Point is also a popular starting point for cross-town moves throughout South Orange County. We regularly move families between Dana Point and nearby cities like San Juan Capistrano, Mission Viejo, and Laguna Beach. For longer moves down the coast to San Diego or north toward Los Angeles, we handle those too. No matter the distance, Sutton Movers brings the same reliable service and transparent pricing that has earned us a 5-star reputation across Southern California.

Frequently Asked Questions About Moving in Dana Point

How much do movers cost in Dana Point?

The cost of movers in Dana Point depends on several factors, including the size of your home, the distance of your move, and which services you need. A studio or one-bedroom apartment move will cost less than a four-bedroom house, and adding packing services or specialty item handling will affect the total. The best way to get an accurate price is to request a free quote from Sutton Movers. We will ask about the details of your move and provide a transparent estimate with no hidden fees.

What areas near Dana Point do you serve?

We serve all of South Orange County and beyond. In addition to Dana Point, we regularly handle moves in San Clemente, Laguna Niguel, Laguna Beach, Mission Viejo, and San Juan Capistrano. We also serve the rest of Orange County, San Diego County, the Inland Empire, and Los Angeles. Whether you are moving across town or across the region, Sutton Movers can help.

Do you handle condo and apartment moves in Dana Point?

Yes. A large portion of our Dana Point moves involve condos and apartments, especially in the communities near the harbor and along the coast. Our team is experienced with elevator buildings, narrow hallways, and the specific requirements that many HOAs have for move-in and move-out days. If your building requires a certificate of insurance (COI), reserved elevator time, or floor and wall protection, we handle all of that as part of the job.

Can you move us from Dana Point to San Diego?

Absolutely. We handle long-distance moves throughout Southern California, including Dana Point to San Diego and everywhere in between. The route along the I-5 corridor and Pacific Coast Highway is one we drive regularly. Whether you are heading to North County San Diego, downtown, or the South Bay, Sutton Movers will get your belongings there safely and on schedule.

Do you provide packing services for Dana Point moves?

Yes, we offer full packing and unpacking services. Our team brings all the materials, including boxes, tape, bubble wrap, and paper, and packs everything from kitchenware to closets. We also handle specialty items like surfboards, framed artwork, mirrors, and antiques with extra care. If you prefer to pack yourself and just need moving labor, that works too.
