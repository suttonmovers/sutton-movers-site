Corona Movers

Moving to or within Corona? Known as the Circle City, Corona is one of the most popular residential areas in the Inland Empire, with neighborhoods ranging from the established communities near Grand Boulevard and Main Street to newer master-planned developments like Dos Lagos and Eagle Glen. Sutton Movers provides dependable, professional moving services throughout Corona and the surrounding region, whether you are relocating across town or coming from Orange County or San Diego.

Corona's diverse neighborhoods each come with their own moving considerations. Hillside homes in Sierra Del Oro and Skyline Heights often have steep driveways, tight turnarounds, and long carries from the truck to the front door. Older neighborhoods in South Corona and the areas near downtown can have narrow streets and limited parking that require careful truck placement. Communities like Chase Ranch, Corona Hills, and North Corona feature HOA-managed properties where move-in windows may be restricted to specific days or hours. Our crew plans for all of this ahead of time so your move stays on schedule.

Corona sits at the crossroads of two major freeways, the 91 and the I-15, making it a natural hub for moves between the Inland Empire, Orange County, and greater Los Angeles. We regularly handle relocations from Corona to cities along the 91 corridor like Anaheim, Fullerton, and Riverside, as well as moves south along the I-15 toward Temecula, Murrieta, and San Diego. Whether your move is 5 miles or 50, we bring the same level of preparation and care.

Sutton Movers offers both full-service moves and labor-only assistance in Corona. Full-service moves include a truck, all equipment, and a trained crew to load, transport, and unload your belongings. If you have rented your own truck or moving container, our labor-only option provides the manpower to do the heavy lifting while you handle the driving. Both options include furniture disassembly and reassembly, floor and doorway protection, and careful handling of fragile items.

We also serve businesses in Corona's commercial district and surrounding office parks. Whether you are moving a small office, a retail location, or a warehouse operation, our team works around your schedule to minimize downtime. We handle cubicle systems, IT equipment, filing cabinets, and other commercial furniture with the same attention we give to residential moves. Call us at (619) 602-3000 for a free quote on your Corona move.
