Moving to Perris? Located in the heart of the Inland Empire, Perris offers some of the most affordable housing in Riverside County along with wide-open spaces and quick freeway access to surrounding cities. Sutton Movers provides dependable, full-service moving throughout Perris and the greater Riverside County area.

Perris features a diverse mix of property types that each present unique moving needs. Downtown Perris has older single-family homes and small commercial buildings along established streets. The Perris Valley area includes newer tract-home developments with two-story layouts and attached garages. Properties adjacent to March Air Reserve Base often house military families on tight relocation timelines, and the rural eastern portions of Perris feature large-lot properties, horse setups, and agricultural land where long driveways and unpaved roads require careful truck planning.

Our service area extends well beyond Perris. We regularly handle moves to and from Menifee, Moreno Valley, and Hemet, as well as longer-distance relocations throughout Southern California. Whether you are moving across town or across the region, we bring the same level of care and professionalism.

Summer heat in the Perris Valley can be intense, with temperatures frequently exceeding 105 degrees. We schedule early-morning start times during the hotter months to keep both our crew and your belongings safe. For rural properties, we bring the right equipment to handle gravel driveways, narrow gates, and longer carry distances from the truck to the front door.

Every Perris move comes with transparent pricing, professional packing materials, furniture protection, and a crew that shows up on time. Reach out today for a free quote and see why families across the Perris Valley choose Sutton Movers.

Frequently Asked Questions About Moving in Perris

How much do movers charge in Perris, CA?

Local moves in Perris typically cost between $350 and $1,100 depending on home size, total items, and distance. Rural properties with long driveways or difficult access may require additional logistics. We provide free, no-obligation quotes so you know the cost before moving day.

What areas near Perris do you serve?

We serve all cities surrounding Perris including Menifee, Moreno Valley, Hemet, Lake Perris, and the March ARB area. We also handle long-distance moves to San Diego, Orange County, and Los Angeles.

Can you move rural or large-lot properties in Perris?

Yes. We have experience with the rural properties on the east side of Perris, including homes on unpaved roads, equestrian setups, and properties with limited truck access. We bring the right equipment and plan the route in advance to keep your move on schedule.

Does Sutton Movers carry insurance for moves?

Yes. All moves include basic cargo coverage at no additional cost. We also offer upgraded full-value protection for customers who want comprehensive coverage on high-value items. Your move coordinator will walk you through the options when you book.
